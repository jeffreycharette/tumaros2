    Our Team

    Ancient CIty Midwives was founded in 2008 by two local midwives with the vision of creating a medical practice that treats every patient with dignity and first-rate care. The nurses and office staff at Ancient City Midwives are highly-trained and exceptionally friendly. We understand that for many, visiting a healthcare provider can be a stressful experience. Our comfortable surroundings and compassionate medical professionals will ease your fears

    • Michele Rogero, CNM, ARNP

      Michele was born and raised here in the nation's oldest city! During her 10 years as a labor and delivery nurse, she worked toward her dream of becoming a midwife. In 2002, she earned her BSN from the University of Central Florida in Orlando. In 2005, she earned her MSN form Regis University in Denver, Colorado. In 2008, she graduated from the University of Florida's Nurse-Midwifery Program. (Gooooo Gators!)

      Michele's friends and family call her Missy. She is married with four wonderful children. In her free time, she enjoys fishing, boating and spending time with her family.

    • Margie

      Our Office Manager and Receptionist Margie Jones is the proud mother of four children, one of whom is Michele Rogero (Missy); and a proud grandmother of nine! She’s lived in Saint Augustine for forty years and has been working with Ancient City Midwives since 2008. Margie loves getting to know patients, but her favorite perk is meeting the newborns! If you’re calling in to our office, most likely you will be hearing Margie’s pleasant voice on the other end of the line.

    • Jennifer

      Jennifer Atehortua was born in Colombia and speaks both Spanish and English fluently. She is working on becoming tri-lingual as she is currently learning American Sign Language also! Jennifer has been a medical assistant at Ancient City Midwives since 2010, when Missy delivered Jennifer’s second child. She enjoyed the experience so much that she decided she wanted to work alongside Missy and be a part of other mother’s wonderful experiences. Jennifer lives in Saint Augustine with her husband of ten years and thier two children. Jennifer’s gentle and cheerful manner is a delight to patients and coworkers alike!

    • Lindy

      A medical assistant since 1996, Lindy Babb is somewhat new to our office; but she’s had the benefit of working with Missy several years ago. A mother of five children and grandmother of three, Lindy recently returned to our fair city after moving away in 2002. She and her husband look forward to reestablishing connections here with their friends and family in Saint Augustine!
