ARE TODAY’S GROWTH INVESTORS MISSING THE BIG PICTURE?

“One of the hallmarks of capitalism is innovation, and in times of crisis and uncertainty, it’s often difficult to see the potential directly in front of us.”1
Over the past three years, equity markets have ebbed and flowed as investors have weighed the prospects of continued economic growth against the backdrop of global uncertainty.
Add this to the fact that advisors are inundated every day with information on the latest investment opportunities, making it especially challenging to step back and see the big picture.
How can advisors cut through the noise and uncertainty to take an informed, active approach to investing?


Growth Investing: Opportunity One Stock at a Time, a new white paper from UBS Global Asset Management,2 helps shed light on:
  • The current landscape of U.S. industries and companies — and what future winners may look like
  • The value of active management in understanding the
    drivers behind future earnings growth
  • The risk of ignoring long-term opportunities for fear of
    short-term declines
Get the full picture by downloading the paper today.
