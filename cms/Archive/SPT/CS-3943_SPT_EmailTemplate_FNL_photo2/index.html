Service Options


Schwab Performance Technologies is committed to supporting you through regulatory changes that impact your portfolio accounting needs and we are pleased to announce the new cost basis reconciliation services available to PortfolioServices clients.

The value in reconciling cost basis within PortfolioServices is that it allows your records to match your custodian’s data so you can use it as the source of accurate gain/loss reporting and trading for your clients.

Select Your Option by March 31

There are three cost basis reconciliation service options from which to choose and we require your selection by March 31. Please be advised that if we do not receive your selection by March 31, we will assume your firm opts out of all cost basis tracking services. If you decide to opt-in for cost basis reconciliation after March 31, your reconciliation will begin at the time of your request and will not be backdated to an earlier date.

Choose One of the Following Options for Your Firm

  • Opt Out—Your decision to track cost basis in PortfolioServices is completely optional and you may elect to opt out of all cost basis tracking services. The performance reporting and billing services we provide you today will remain unchanged.
  • Opt In to Cost Basis Reconciliation with Automated Reset— Our system will automatically reset your cost basis to match your custodian’s systems as of a specific date. We will reconcile accounts using the cost basis download files from primary custodians and there is no additional fee for this service.

Our goal is to provide you with industry-leading solutions and we will continue to update our platform as new phases of the cost basis legislation take effect over the coming years. Thank you for working with Schwab Performance Technologies.
