MANAGING RISK IN GLOBAL
CAPITAL MARKETS

You can strive to take advantage of global investment opportunities while attempting to effectively manage risk—
if you know what to look for. Join our webcast, Perspectives on Opportunity and Risk in an Interconnected World, with Stephen J. Cucchiaro, chief investment officer of Windhaven Investment Management, for a review of the global capital markets and risks to consider.
In addition to a discussion of rising interest rates, the European debt crisis, and geopolitical uncertainty in the Middle East, Mr. Cucchiaro will:
  • Discuss the importance of preserving capital in a down market
  • Show how different market events can provide opportunities for certain asset classes to outperform others
  • Share insights into the importance of global diversification and how it can help minimize risk
We hope you’ll be able to join us.
