About

Overview

Hatch, co-founded by Katie Jain and Joel Templin, is a branding and graphic design firm centered on one powerful idea: the best design is honest, hands-on and human. Hatch helps new products and business get off the ground, and helps existing brands take on new life. Since opening our doors, Hatch has created brand identity, packaging, collateral, advertising, promotional and interactive design for global companies including American Eagle Outfitters, Apple, Coca-Cola, Harrah’s, Hilton, Starbucks and many others.

Hatch Design’s work has been recognized for excellence in virtually every major branding and design competition around the world. Our principals are regularly invited to address design, advertising, educational, and business organizations across the U.S., and are frequent judges of design competitions. We have recently received an important distinction from the graphic design publication, Graphis, as one of the “Top 45 Branding Agencies in the U.S.”
