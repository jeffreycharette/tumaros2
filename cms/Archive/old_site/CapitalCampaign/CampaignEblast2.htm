Campaign Update

Dear Friends,

The lights in the gallery may be off but the ICA’s future couldn’t be brighter.

Eight months have passed since the ICA became the proud owner of the 7500 square-foot building at 560 South First Street, thanks to generous gifts from the ICA's Board of Directors, the City of San Jose’s Redevelopment Agency and an anonymous donor. Renovation work is now underway.

On January 12, 2007, the ICA held an evening reception to celebrate. It was great to see so many friends, donors, elected officials, and volunteers join us for the event. It was inspiring to hear Mayor Chuck Reed talk about how important the ICA is to the City of San Jose. Click here to see all of the photos.

ICA’s capital campaign is critical to our future.

The capital campaign will fund the transformation of the facility into a dynamic regional arts center, enhancing our position as a West Coast leader in the presentation of contemporary art. Additionally, it will ensure the ICA’s future sustainability through facility ownership.

Our campaign has been enthusiastically received, and we are confident of reaching our $4,200,000 goal. To date, we have raised over $2M. We are extremely grateful to those of you who have already made significant, thoughtful gifts. The rest of our campaign will focus on reaching out to many more of our supporters and partners---foundations, members, volunteers, and others who have an interest in the future of the ICA.

In June 2007, the ICA will re-open !

The construction crew has begun work and is making steady progress on the renovation.

We will reopen with double the exhibition space, a Reading/ Resource room for artists and collectors, a new media project room and a Printmaking Center. During construction, the galleries are closed and the administrative offices have been temporarily relocated. We can still be contacted at 408.283.8155.

We are planning a week-long series of opening events in early June. Mark your calendars and stay tuned for details. To get more involved, please call us!

Cathy Kimball Karen Tucker
Executive Director Campaign Chair
