Directions to the ICA by Roadway

The San Jose ICA office and gallery is located at 560 South First Street. There is metered street parking along first and a parking lot underneath Hwy 280 ($3 all day) 1 block south of the ICA. The nearest cross streets are Reed and William Streets.

From Highway 101 – Southbound (from San Francisco, Burlingame, San Mateo, Palo Alto, etc.)
Take Hwy 101 South to Downtown San Jose
Take Guadelupe Parkway/Highway 87 Exit (South)
Take Park Avenue/San Carlos Street exit, turn Left onto Park Avenue
Go 2 blocks to Almaden Boulevard, turn Right
Go 1 block to San Carlos Street, turn Left
Go 2 blocks to South First Street, turn Right
The ICA is 2 ½ blocks down the street on the left.

From Highway 101 – Northbound (from Morgan Hill, Gilroy, Hollister, Los Angeles, etc.)

Take Hwy 101 North to San Jose
Exit on the Hwy 280 North(Downtown San Jose)
Take Hwy 87 North to the San Carlos Street Exit
Go Right on San Carlosto South First Street, turn Right
The ICA is 2 ½ blocks down the street on the left.

From Highway 280 – Southbound (from San Francisco, Half Moon Bay, Stanford, Los Altos, etc.)

Take Hwy 280 South to Downtown San Jose
Take the 7 th StreetExit
Stay to the right following the signs for 82 to First Street, go Right
Continue 3 blocks to 560 South First Street; the ICA is on the right.
