Directions to the ICA:

The San Jose ICA is located at 560 South First Street. There is metered (meters operate until 6pm) street parking along South First street and a parking lot underneath Hwy 280 ($5 all day) one block south of the ICA. The nearest cross streets are Reed and William Streets.

Google Map of the ICA

From Highway 101-Southbound (from San Francisco, San Mateo, Palo Alto, etc.)
Take Hwy 101 South to Downtown San Jose
Take Guadelupe Parkway/Highway 87 Exit (South)
Merge onto Hwy 280 (South)
Take the Virginia St. exit toward Hwy 82 / 7th St.
Take a slight Right (towards Hwy 82) onto E. Virginia St.
Turn Right on South First St.
The ICA is 2½ blocks up the street, stay to the right.

From Highway 101-Northbound (from Morgan Hill, Gilroy, Hollister, Los Angeles, etc.)
Take Hwy 101 North to San Jose
Exit on the Hwy 280 North (Downtown San Jose)
Take Hwy 87 North to the San Carlos Street Exit
Go Right on San Carlos to South First Street, turn Right
The ICA is 2 ½ blocks down the street on the left.

From Highway 280-Southbound (From San Francisco, Stanford, Los Altos, etc.)
Take Hwy 280 South to Downtown San Jose
Take the Virginia St. exit toward Hwy 82 / 7th St.
Take a slight Right (towards Hwy 82) onto E. Virginia St.
Turn Right on South First St.
The ICA is 2½ blocks up the street, stay to the right.

From Highway 280-Northbound
Take Hwy 87 North (Downtown San Jose) to the San Carlos Street Exit
Go Right on San Carlos to South First Street, turn Right
The ICA is 2 ½ blocks down the street on the left.

From Highway 680-Southbound (from Sacramento, Concord, Walnut Creek, Milpitas, etc.)
Take Hwy 680 South to San Jose
Continue on Hwy 680, it will become Hwy 280 North (downtown San Jose)
Take Hwy 87 North (Downtown San Jose) to the San Carlos Street Exit
Go Right on San Carlos to South First Street, turn Right
The ICA is 2 ½ blocks down the street on the left.

From Highway 17-Northbound (From Santa Cruz, Los Gatos, etc.)
Take Hwy 17 North to Downtown San Jose
Exit Hwy 280 South to Downtown San Jose
Take the Virginia St. exit toward Hwy 82 / 7th St.
Take a slight Right (towards Hwy 82) onto E. Virginia St.
Turn Right on South First St.
The ICA is 2½ blocks up the street, stay to the right.

From Highway 880-Southbound (from Berkeley, Oakland, Hayward, Fremont, Milpitas, etc.)
Take Hwy 880 South to San Jose
Exit Hwy 280 South (Downtown San Jose)
Take the Virginia St. exit toward Hwy 82 / 7th St.
Take a slight Right (towards Hwy 82) onto E. Virginia St.
Turn Right on South First St.
The ICA is 2½ blocks up the street, stay to the right.
