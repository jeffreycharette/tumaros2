Give the Gift of Art This Holiday Season

Gift an ICA membership to a friend, teacher or family member. An ICA Friend membership provides complimentary admission to our Annual Fall Auction - one of the South Bay's hottest cultural events of the year. Check out the complete list of membership benefits.

Gift a print workshop certificate to the art-maker on your list. The '09 ICA Print Center workshop calendar is chock-full of new and exciting classes.

Gift an ICA exhibition catalogue. Browse our selection of catalogues and artist monographs. They make wonderful gifts for everyone on your holiday list. A limited number of Manuel Neri monographs, signed by the artist, are available for sale. ICA members receive a 10% discount.

Call or email or stop by to purchase your art gifts. We'll wrap up all the details for you.
