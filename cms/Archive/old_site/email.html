The Gallery Game, Thursday, 9/12/02

Over the years, the gallery system has become the primary means by which artists reach the public. This session will look at the structure of the art world from the perspective of the art dealer and the artist.
Reception: 6 – 7 pm Discussion: 7 – 9pm
Panelists: Todd Hosfelt, Katrina Traywick, Heather Marks, Michele Scott, and Fred Spratt.
