The 28th Annual Fall Art Auction
Going Once, Going Twice, Gone
But not forgotten



Dear All,

We wish to extend our most sincere thanks to our members, board, guests, contributing artists, and sponsors for making our 28th Annual Fall Art Auction an unbelievable success. We are so very pleased to announce that we exceeded our fundraising goals. We are speechless. Yes, speechless, us. Imagine that.

It was a magical evening. The auction gallery was filled with enthusiastic bidders (and snappy dressers!), outstanding artwork, fabulous food, drink, and an infectious energy that continued to build throughout the evening. We all had such a terrific time.

Our upcoming exhibition and program schedule is engaging, diverse, and enticing. Together with the bounty of our auction proceeds and the encouragement of our dedicated and talented community of supporters, we know that the best is yet to come. Thank you all.

Most sincerely,

Cathy Kimball and the ICA Staff
