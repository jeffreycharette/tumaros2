Photographer Unknown, Young Female Dancer, tintype (hand colored), 1865, Courtesy of Stephen and Connie Wirtz

 

Opening in July, an exhibition of vintage prints from the private collection of San Francisco gallerist Stephen Wirtz and guest curated by David Pace, lecturer, Art and Art History Department, Santa Clara University and ICA Board member. These images will also provide a lens to examine how contemporary photographers are informed by the historic work, whether they rely on its nostalgia and sentimentality or create radically different imagery.
