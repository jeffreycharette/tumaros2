Our dear friend, Michele Scott, passed away unexpectedly on February 24th. Michele was a vibrant, enthusiastic and essential part of the ICA. She was a Board member for six years and had been involved with the Monotype Marathon since its inception 14 years ago. She often helped out at the front desk and was the face of the gallery and the first point of contact to many.

In tribute to Michele's accomplishments as an artist, a selection of her prints will be included in an upcoming exhibition at Bill Gould Design. A limited number of prints will be available for purchase, with 100% of the proceeds directed towards our efforts to name the ICA Print Center in Michele's honor.

A public reception for the exhibition will be held Wednesday, May 7th, 2008, from 4:00 pm to 9:00 pm.

Bill Gould Design is located at 394-A Umbarger Road, San Jose, CA 95111. For more information about the exhibition or reception, please contact Bill Gould Design at 408-224-9890.
