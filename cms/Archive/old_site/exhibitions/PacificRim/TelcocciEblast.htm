Gina Telcocci
6:30 pm

 
 

Gina Telcocci
Trap, 2004

  
In conjunction with the current exhibition, In the Round: Selections from the Pacific Rim Sculptors Group, every Thursday evening artists in the exhibition will be on hand in the gallery to discuss his or her work. This week's featured artist is Gina Telcocci. Admission is free. Please join us.

Working with wood, reed, wax, and seeds. Gina Telcocci weaves organic forms from organic materials. The materials themselves suggest ideas, forms, and ways of working which contribute to the total effect and concept of the pieces. Although Telcocci works intuitively, all aspects of the pieces are carefully considered and chosen.Baskets, which she considers the perfect combination of simplicity and complexity, continue to inspire her work as seen in her skylight installation at the ICA. Her goal is to create mysterious, beautiful and whimsical shapes that transform an environment.
