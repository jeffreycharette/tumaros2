Ann Weber
6:30 pm

Ann Weber
Almost, 2004
In conjunction with the current exhibition, In the Round: Selections from the Pacific Rim Sculptors Group, every Thursday evening artists in the exhibition will be on hand in the gallery to discuss his or her work. This week's featured artist is Ann Weber. Admission is free. Please join us.

After years of enduring the physically demanding process of sculpting large forms from heavy clay, Ann Weber discovered that simple, lightweight cardboard and staples offered unlimited possibilities as materials for creating the organic forms she envisioned. Using cardboard Weber is now able to make very large work that is light and portable, but retains the monumental quality of her ceramic sculptures. Weber’s totemic abstract figures suggest humor, irony and whimsy in an unexpected media making them all the more fun to investigate.
