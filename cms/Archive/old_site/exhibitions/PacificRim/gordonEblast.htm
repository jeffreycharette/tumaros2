Nancy Nodelman , Found and Lost VII, 2004,

In conjunction with the current exhibition, In the Round: Selections from the Pacific Rim Sculptors Group, every Thursday evening artists in the exhibition will be on hand in the gallery to discuss his or her work. This week's featured artists are nancy Ziegler Nodelman and Sara Gordon. Admission is free. Please join us.
       

   
  Sara Gordon is interested in telling stories about women’s lives through the image of the female form. Over time, she has developed a unique technique of working with hog gut that has allowed her to create freestanding sculptures that retain their form.    

left:
Nancy Nodelman
Retrogression, 1998

Nancy Nodelman restructures the content and intent of everyday objects into elegant art forms. Drawing on the tradition of trompe l’oeil, Nodelman recycles found, industrial, and prosaic components into forms that challenge their identity.
