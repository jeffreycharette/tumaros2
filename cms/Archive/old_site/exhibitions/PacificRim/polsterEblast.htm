Laurie Polster, Vessels, 2004

Laurie Polster
Thursday, January 27th
6:30 pm
Vessels, Installation details
In conjunction with the current exhibition, In the Round: Selections from the Pacific Rim Sculptors Group, every Thursday evening one of the artists in the exhibition will be on hand in the gallery to discuss his or her work. This week's featured artist is Laurie Polster. Admission is free. Please join us.
Laurie Polster has created a site-specific installation, Vessels, for this exhibition. Working with a variety of materials, she explores issues of the natural and constructed world in order to explore the boundaries and connections between them. She incorporates natural materials she has collected as well as construction materials she amassed when she built her own studio.
