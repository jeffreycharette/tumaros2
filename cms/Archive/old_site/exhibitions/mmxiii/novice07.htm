The Novice Workshops

Saturday, June 2 and Saturday June 16
10AM - 2 PM

Want to learn more about the monotype process?
On Saturday, June 2 and Saturday June 16 from 10am to 2pm, the ICA will hold workshops just for beginners at its brand new print center. Master printmakers Fanny Retsek and Mercy Smullen will lead participants through the techniques of making a monotype. You will have the opportunity to create a print and take it home with you. All materials (including lunch) will be provided by the ICA.

Workshop fee $200.

Call 283-8155 for reservations.
