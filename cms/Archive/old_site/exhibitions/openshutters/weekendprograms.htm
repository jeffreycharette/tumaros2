NOVEMBER 10, 2007

Creating Open Shutters Iraq



Panel Discussion 3 - 5 pm

Eugenie Dolberg will lead a discussion about the challenges she and the participants faced in order to realize the Open Shutters Iraq project. Two of the exhibiting photographers will elaborate on their images and life in Iraq today.

Open Shutters Iraq is a project of Iraq: Reframe a Montalvo Arts Center Initiative.

NOVEMBER 11, 2007

The Landscape of War: Transformations of the Familiar

Panel Discussion 3 - 5 pm
Booksigning and reception 5 - 6 pm

How do fear and violence shape the landscapes through which we move and how does secrecy hide government's actions from its citizens? Moderated by author Rebecca Solnit, the discussion will contemplate how we can reclaim public space and create meaningful resistance in this time of war.

Panelists include: Trevor Paglen, exhibiting artist and author of Torture Taxi: On the Trail of the CIA's Rendition Flights, Salam Hassan, former Pacifica radio correspondent in Baghdad, Kenneth Helphand, author of Defiant Gardens: Making Gardens in Wartime, Michael Light, exhibiting artist
