Looking for something different for your next event? Tired of sterile meeting rooms and banquet halls decorated in 1982? The San Jose Institute of Contemporary Art offers a creative alternative. Host your clients, staff, VIPs, or special guests in a unique atmosphere surrounded by changing exhibitions of the finest contemporary art. The gallery can accommodate groups of up to 200 for a reception, or up to 100 for a sit-down dinner (depending on the exhibition). Call Liz Waldo at 408.283.8155 for information on rental fees and available dates.
