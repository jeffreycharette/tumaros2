The newly renovated San Jose Institute of Contemporary Art is the centerpiece of downtown San Jose’s burgeoning SoFA arts and entertainment district. This unique and sophisticated setting, enhanced by ICA’s art exhibitions, is an ideal event venue for weekend and evening cocktail receptions, business meetings, club and social events. The 3,500 square foot event facility accommodates gatherings for up to 200 guests. A Conference Room is also available to rent for private daytime events, Monday through Friday, during the ICA’s regular public gallery hours (10am to 5pm).
