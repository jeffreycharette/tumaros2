FACILITY RENTAL

The San Jose Institute of Contemporary Art’s newly renovated facility at 560 South First Street is the centerpiece of downtown San Jose’s burgeoning SoFA Arts District. With over 3,500 square feet of gallery space, the ICA offers an upscale and unique setting that can accommodate evening events for as many as 175 guests. In addition, the ICA’s conference room offers an ideal setting for daytime meetings, luncheons and seminars for up to 12 people.

Private Evening Use
The ICA’s galleries are available for two to three hour evening cocktail receptions. Rental fees will vary depending on the logistics and design of each event. 

Conference Room Use
The Conference Room is available for private daytime events Monday through Friday during normal business hours. Rental times include set-up and clean-up, but unfortunately cannot extend beyond 5:00 pm.
