Focus Gallery
This Show Needs You
March 28 - May 17, 2008
Opening Reception First Friday: April 4, 6pm - late

 This Show Needs You relies on the willingness of audiences to co-author the work. Throughout the exhibition, workshops, performances and public programs will allow opportunities to engage with the artists and ideas of this participatory exhibition.

Check out Related Public Programs (Including info about the lemon quarantine and much more).

This exhibition is in conjunction with University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC) conference, Intervene! Interrupt! Rethinking Art as Social Practice from May 15-17.
