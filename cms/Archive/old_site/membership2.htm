Exciting ICA Member News: More Art for your Membership Buck!

The ICA is now a member of The North American Reciprocal Museum Program (NARM) - an association of more than 400 museums in North America offering select membership benefits to other qualifying museum members.  If you are a member of the ICA at the Friend level and above, you are entitled to the following privileges at participating museums:
*Free/member admission during regular museum hours
*Member discounts at museum shops
*Discounts on concert/lectures tickets. 

You must present your ICA membership card validated with a gold NARM sticker to take advantage of this membership benefit.  

Contact the ICA to receive your validated NARM membership card (Friend + members), or get more information on the NARM program.

Download list of participating NARM institutions.
