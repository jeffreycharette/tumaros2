From Highway 101 - Northbound
(from Morgan Hill, Gilroy, Hollister, Los Angeles, etc.)

  1. Take Hwy 101 North to San Jose
  2. Exit on the Hwy 280 North (Downtown San Jose)
  3. Take Hwy 87 North to the San Carlos Street Exit
  4. Go Right on San Carlos and turn Right at Woz Way
  5. Continue on Woz Way as it becomes Balbach Street after crossing Almaden Blvd.
  6. Balbach Street dead ends mid-block at the ICA's back door Make a left or right turn and go around the block to the South First Street entrance.
