From Highway 101 - Southbound
(from San Francisco, Burlingame, San Mateo, Palo Alto, etc.)

  1. Take Hwy 101 South to Downtown San Jose
  2. Take Guadelupe Parkway/Highway 87 Exit (South)
  3. Take Park Avenue/San Carlos Street exit, turn Left onto Park Avenue
  4. Go 2 blocks to Almaden Boulevard, turn Right
  5. Go 1 block to San Carlos Street, turn Left
  6. Go 2 blocks to South First Street, turn Right
  7. The ICA is 1 ? blocks down the street on the right.
  8. From Highway 101 - Northbound (from Morgan Hill, Gilroy, Hollister, Los Angeles, etc.)
  9. Take Hwy 101 North to San Jose
  10. Exit on the Hwy 280 North (Downtown San Jose)
  11. Take Hwy 87 North to the San Carlos Street Exit
  12. Go Right on San Carlos and turn Right at Woz Way
  13. Continue on Woz Way as it becomes Balbach Street after crossing Almaden Blvd.
  14. Balbach Street dead ends mid-block at the ICA's back door
  15. Make a left or right turn and go around the block to the South First Street entrance.
