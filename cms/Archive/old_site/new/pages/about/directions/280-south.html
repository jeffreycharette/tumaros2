From Highway 280 - Southbound
(from San Francisco, Half Moon Bay, Stanford, Los Altos, etc.)

  1. Take Hwy 280 South to Downtown San Jose
  2. Take Vine Street/Almaden Boulevard Exit
  3. Stay to the left, cross Vine Street (light), going Left onto Almaden Blvd. (under freeway)
  4. Go 2 blocks and make a Right at Balbach Street
  5. Go 2 blocks and Balbach dead ends mid-block at the ICA's back door
  6. Make a left or right turn and go around the block to the South First Street entrance
