Selections from Print Center Workshops
are on view at the ICA through October 25th, 2008

Photoploymer workshop participants create intaglio plates from their own drawings, photos or digital imagery. The plates are developed with light and water and are printed as traditional etchings on paper.

Come check out prints that highlight this season's Print Center workshops. Works in monotype, etching, photopolymer and more are exhibited. See examples and learn about the processes available at our studio.

Participating artists are Karen Chew, Spencer Hale, Sieglinde Van Damme and Alayne Yellum.
