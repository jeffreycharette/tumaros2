Photopolymer Intaglio
August 11 - 15, Monday - Friday, 10 - 4:30,
$500. Instructor: Fanny Retsek
Create etchings using photographic positives, drawings on transparent media or digital images. This new water-soluble non-toxic method is a wonderful alternative to etching and traditional photogravure. It also can be used successfully in fusing digital print technology with traditional printing practices.

Monotype, all levels welcome
August 23 & 24, Saturday and Sunday,
10 - 4:30, $270. Instructor: Fanny Retsek
Learn or improve basic monotype techniques while also learning about inks, drawing tools, paper, chine collé, and xerox transfer. In this multi-level workshop personal attention will be given to your independent projects.

Solar Plate Etching with world-renowned printmaker Dan Welden.
September 14, 15 & 16, Sunday - Tuesday,
10 - 4:30, $495
Learn "Acidless Etching" with Master Printmaker, Dan Welden, pioneer of safer printmaking practices. This environmentally friendly approach is for any artist involved with drawing, painting or photography. For more information on Dan Welden click here to link to our website.
