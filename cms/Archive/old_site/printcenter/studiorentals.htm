The Michele J. Scott Open Access Program

The ICA Print Center is an open-access studio created for the community.

The ICA Print Center is available for experienced printmakers to use the studio and press for only $15 per hour. Artists must be members of the ICA (artist membership rate is only $35 a year).Call the ICA or email Fanny Retsek to make an appoinment for your required certification and orientation.

2010 Open Access Days:

Printing and Copper Etching: $15/hour
Sundays from 10:00 - 5:00 on the following dates:

March 7, April 11 (the first Sunday in April is Easter), May 2, June 6, August 1, September 12 (the first Sunday in September falls on Labor Day weekend), October 3, November 7, and December 5

The ICA Print Center is also available for private rental.
Rent the ICA Print Center for groups of 1 to 6 printmakers beginning at $50/hour .

The ICA Print Center is an opportunity to work with a Master Printer.
Private and small group instruction and project realization with the Master Printer are available by appointment beginning at $50/hour.

Please contact Fanny Retsek, Print Center Director for more information on any of these programs or to make an appointment. Email fanny@sjica.org or call 408.283.8155.
