The Reading Resource Room aka The Lounge
Filled with reference books, monographs, and contemporary art journals, the ICA's Lounge is available for public use, free of charge during regular gallery hours. The Lounge was created to provide resources for gallery visitors to make connections between our exhibitions and contemporary culture. We also offer a large selection of video programs featuring artist interviews. Visitors are welcome to browse the video library to watch programming on a flat-screen TV. A community bulletin board provides up-to-date information on the arts and events happening around the Bay Area.
