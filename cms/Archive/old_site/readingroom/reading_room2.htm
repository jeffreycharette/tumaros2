The Reading/Resource Room at the ICA

Come read and look at the ICA! The Reading/Resource room is a space to make connections. With each exhibition the ICA carries books on featured artists to further deepen the understanding of their work and artistic practice. This room aspires to grow and change with interests and perspectives of our exhibitions and guests. While many of its shelves are not yet full, we are excited about their potential to be filled.

Spend some time reading and looking:

Watch the SPARK profiles on Landscape of War artists Trevor Paglen, Mike Arcega, Enrique Chagoya and Sandow Birk.
