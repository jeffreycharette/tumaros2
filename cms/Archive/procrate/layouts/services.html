    About Pro-Crate Custom Commercial Crating

    We at Pro-Crate and Assembly pride ourselves in providing a level of excellence to all of our commercial customers. Pro-Crate’s unique capabilities allow us to work with to provide a level of customization tailored to meeting your shipping needs. We offer a wide variety of services including: solid crates, slatted crates, skeletal crates and custom built skids along with a knowledge and know how of packing and securing your item to eliminate a potential hazard.

    Pro-Crate can either build crates in our warehouse and deliver them to you or build on site in our fully equipped mobile workshops. For extra levels of protection we can vacuum seal your item in a mylar bag to eliminate harmful water and element exposure. Our technicians are experts in the crating and packaging of all types of items from heavy machinery to motorcycles and everything in between.

    Whether the shipping is foreign or domestic Pro-Crate has you covered, we are ISPM 15 certified and able to stamp all crates in order to meet customs requirements. Pro-Crate tailor makes its serves to partner with you in order to fully meet your shipping needs. Let Pro-Crate be your one stop shop for all of your packing and crating services.
