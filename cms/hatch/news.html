Another Colorful Easter
at Hatch

Hatch Celebrates 4Th Annual Egg Coloring Contest

This year, we mailed over 750 egg-coloring kits to clients, friends and associates (and even sold a handful on Facebook). Over 120 colored eggs were submitted to our website with Julie Niemann receiving top votes — your egg-cup trophy is on its way, Julie! Congratulations!

On The Fly, an online men’s retailer with its home base in San Francisco, is passionate about serving the clothing and accessory needs of the busy, modern gentleman. Hatch partnered with its founder to elevate the On The Fly brand to reflect the company’s luxury product offerings — including a collection of premium clothing, home furnishing, personal grooming and cigar brands. As part of the collaboration, Hatch designed a cohesive identity system that featured in-store signage, a completely refreshed website (even an interactive Modern Gentleman's Quiz), business stationery, and gift packaging that evokes an earlier era of charm, sophistication and urbane wit.
